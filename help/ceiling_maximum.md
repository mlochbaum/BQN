# Left Ceiling (`⌈`)

## `⌈ 𝕩`: Ceiling

Round `𝕩` up.

[Pervasive.](../doc/arithmetic.md#pervasion)

          ⌈ 1.2‿π‿3‿7.89



## `𝕨 ⌈ 𝕩`: Maximum

Maximum of `𝕨` and `𝕩`.

[Pervasive.](../doc/arithmetic.md#pervasion)

          1 ⌈ 2

          2‿3 ⌈ 4‿2
