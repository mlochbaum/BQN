Left Ceiling (`⌈`)

`⌈ 𝕩`: Ceiling  

Round `𝕩` up.

[Pervasive.](https://mlochbaum.github.io/BQN/doc/arithmetic.html#pervasion)
```
  ⌈ 1.2‿π‿3‿7.89
⟨ 2 4 3 8 ⟩
```

`𝕨 ⌈ 𝕩`: Maximum

Maximum of `𝕨` and `𝕩`.

[Pervasive.](https://mlochbaum.github.io/BQN/doc/arithmetic.html#pervasion)
```
  1 ⌈ 2
2
  2‿3 ⌈ 4‿2
⟨ 4 3 ⟩ 
```
