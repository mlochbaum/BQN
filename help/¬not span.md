# Not (`¬`)

`¬ 𝕩`: Logical Not  

Logical Not of `𝕩`.

[Pervasive.](https://mlochbaum.github.io/BQN/doc/arithmetic.html#pervasion)

      ¬ 0
    1
      ¬ 1‿0
    ⟨ 0 1 ⟩


`𝕨 ¬ 𝕩`: Span

Count of numbers in the inclusive range from `𝕩` to `𝕨`.

[Pervasive.](https://mlochbaum.github.io/BQN/doc/arithmetic.html#pervasion)

      3 ¬ 1
    3
      3‿4 ¬ 0‿2
    ⟨ 4 3 ⟩
