# Minus (`-`)

## `- 𝕩`: Negate
[→full documentation](../doc/arithmetic.md#basic-arithmetic)

Additive Inverse of `𝕩`.

[Pervasive.](../doc/arithmetic.md#pervasion)

        - 1

        - ¯1


## `𝕨 - 𝕩`: Subtract
[→full documentation](../doc/arithmetic.md#basic-arithmetic)

Subtract `𝕩` from `𝕨`. `𝕨` and `𝕩` can be characters or numbers.

[Pervasive.](../doc/arithmetic.md#pervasion)

        1 - 2

        1 - 2‿3‿4

        'a' - 4

        'b' - 'a'
