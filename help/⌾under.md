Circle Jot (`⌾`)

`𝔽⌾𝔾 𝕩`, `𝕨 𝔽⌾𝔾 𝕩`: Under

- Apply transformation `𝔾` to all arguments
- Apply `𝔽` to the transformed arguments
- Undo transformation `𝔾`

Where `𝔾` must be 

1. A function invertible by `⁼` (Undo)
2. A structural modification
```
   9⌾(1⊸⊑) 1‿2‿3
⟨ 1 9 3 ⟩
   √⁼ (√1) + (√9)
16
   1 +⌾√ 9
16
```
