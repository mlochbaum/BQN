# Star (`⋆`)
    
## `⋆ 𝕩`: Exponential  
    
`e` (Euler's constant) to the power of `𝕩`.
    
[Pervasive.](https://mlochbaum.github.io/BQN/doc/arithmetic.html#pervasion)
    
          ⋆ 0‿1‿2‿3

    
    
## `𝕨 ⋆ 𝕩`: Power
    
`𝕨` to the power of `𝕩`.
    
[Pervasive.](https://mlochbaum.github.io/BQN/doc/arithmetic.html#pervasion)
    
          2 ⋆ 5

          8‿5‿9 ⋆ 2

          2‿3 ⋆ 3‿¯4
