# Full Stop (`.`)

## `ns . name`: Namespace Field
[→full documentation](../doc/namespace.md)

Access a field with name `name` in namespace `ns`. Field must have been exported with `⇐`.

        {a⇐1} . a

        {F⇐-}.F 5
