# Times (`×`)

## `× 𝕩`: Sign
[→full documentation](../doc/arithmetic.md#basic-arithmetic)

Sign of `𝕩`.
- `¯1` if `𝕩 < 0`
- `0` if `𝕩 = 0`
- `1` if `𝕩 > 0`

[Pervasive.](../doc/arithmetic.md#pervasion)

        × ¯5‿0‿5‿1



## `𝕨 × 𝕩`: Multiply
[→full documentation](../doc/arithmetic.md#basic-arithmetic)

`𝕨` multiplied by `𝕩`.

[Pervasive.](../doc/arithmetic.md#pervasion)

        1 × 2
