Left Floor (`⌊`)

`⌊ 𝕩`: Floor  

Round `𝕩` down.

[Pervasive.](https://mlochbaum.github.io/BQN/doc/arithmetic.html#pervasion)
```
  ⌊ 1.2‿π‿3‿7.89
⟨ 1 3 3 7 ⟩
```

`𝕨 ⌊ 𝕩`: Minimum

Minimum of `𝕨` and `𝕩`.

[Pervasive.](https://mlochbaum.github.io/BQN/doc/arithmetic.html#pervasion)
```
  1 ⌊ 2
1
  2‿3 ⌊ 4‿2
⟨ 2 2 ⟩
```
