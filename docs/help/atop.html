Ring Operator (∘)

𝔽∘𝔾 𝕩: Atop

Apply 𝔾 to 𝕩, then apply 𝔽 (𝔽 𝔾 𝕩).

𝔽 and 𝔾 must be monadic.

↗️

5

       - - 5

𝔽 must be monadic, and 𝔾 must be dyadic.
