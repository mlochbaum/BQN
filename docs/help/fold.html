Acute Accent (´)

𝔽´ 𝕩: Fold

→full documentation

Fold over 𝕩 with 𝔽 from right to left i.e. Insert 𝔽 between the elements of 𝕩.

𝕩 must be a simple list (1 = =𝕩).

↗️

6

    1+2+3
6

    -´ 1‿2‿3

    1-2-3
