Right Pointing Double Angle Quotation (»)

» 𝕩: Shift After

Remove the last element of 𝕩, add a cell of fill values to the start of the first axis of 𝕩.

   » 1‿2‿3
⟨ 0 1 2 ⟩
   » 3‿3 ⥊ 9
┌─       
╵ 0 0 0  
  9 9 9  
  9 9 9  
        ┘
