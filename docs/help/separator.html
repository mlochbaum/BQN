Comma (,) and Diamond (⋄)

, or ⋄: Separator

→full documentation

Separates statements in blocks, programs, and arrays. Characters , and ⋄ are interchangeable with each other and with newline.

↗️
    a ← 3 , ⊢ b ← 2
2

    1 ⋄ 2 , 3
3

    ⟨1 , 2 ⋄ 3⟩
⟨ 1 2 3 ⟩

    {1 ⋄ 2 ⋄ 3}
3
