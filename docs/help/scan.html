𝔽` 𝕩: Scan

→full documentation

Scan over 𝕩 with 𝔽 from left to right, producing intermediate values.

↗️
    +` 1‿2‿3
⟨ 1 3 6 ⟩

    ⟨1, 1+2, (1+2)+3⟩
⟨ 1 3 6 ⟩

    -` 1‿2‿3
⟨ 1 ¯1 ¯4 ⟩

    ⟨1, 1-2, (1-2)-3⟩
⟨ 1 ¯1 ¯4 ⟩
