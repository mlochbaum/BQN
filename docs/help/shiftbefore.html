𝕨 « 𝕩: Shift Before

→full documentation

Remove the first ≠𝕨 (length) major cells from 𝕩, join 𝕨 to the end of 𝕩. Ranks must match.

↗️

⟨ 3 8 5 ⟩

    a ← 3‿3 ⥊ 9

    1‿2‿3 « a
