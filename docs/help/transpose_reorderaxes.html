Circle Backslash (⍉)

⍉ 𝕩: Transpose

→full documentation

Move the first axis of 𝕩 to the end.

↗️
    a ← 3‿3 ⥊ ↕9

    ⍉ a
┌─       
╵ 0 3 6  
  1 4 7  
  2 5 8  
        ┘

    b ← 1‿2‿3 ⥊ ↕6

    ≢⍉ b
⟨ 2 3 1 ⟩

𝕨 ⍉ 𝕩: Reorder Axes

→full documentation

Rearrange the axes of 𝕩 as per the axis indices in 𝕨.

↗️
    ≢ c ← 2‿3‿4‿5‿6 ⥊1
⟨ 2 3 4 5 6 ⟩

    ≢ 1‿3‿2‿0‿4 ⍉ c
⟨ 5 2 4 3 6 ⟩
